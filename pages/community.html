UA TOM

[Univesity of Alabama Tikkun Olam Makers]

Tikkun Olam Makers University of Alabama Chapter, is a student organization dedicated to engineering low-cost, open-source assistive devices, has garnered global recognition for its innovative approach to accessible technology.


Rise Project
We are currently collaborating with educators at a the Rise Center, Tuscaloosa to design an interactive educational game for children with varying abilities. I’m using SolidWorks to create 3D-printable components that make learning more engaging and hands-on for students.


Fall Detection Device
Currently collaborating with a multidisciplinary team to design and develop a wearable fall detection device for older adults. The prototype will use an Arduino with built-in motion sensors to detect sudden impacts and abnormal movement patterns. I’m working on integrating the Arduino with additional hardware components and contributing to the ongoing embedded system design and testing.
